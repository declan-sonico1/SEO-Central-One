# 56 Google Search Statistics to Bookmark for 2024

If you’re curious about the state of Google search in 2024, look no further.

Each year we pick, vet, and categorize a list of up-to-date statistics to give you insights from trusted sources on Google search trends.

1.  Google has a web index of “about 400 billion documents”. ([The Capitol Forum](https://thecapitolforum.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/101823-USA-v-Google-PM.pdf))
2.  Google’s search index is over 100 million gigabytes in size. ([Google](https://www.google.com/search/howsearchworks/how-search-works/organizing-information/))
3.  There are an estimated 3.5 billion searches on Google each day. ([Internet Live Stats](https://www.internetlivestats.com/google-search-statistics/))
4.  61.5% of desktop searches and 34.4% of mobile searches result in no clicks. ([SparkToro](https://sparktoro.com/blog/google-ctr-in-2018-paid-organic-no-click-searches/))  
    ![61.5% of desktop searches result in no clicks.](https://ahrefs.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/js-desktop-clicks-google-2016-2018-1024x617-1-680x410.png)
5.  15% of all Google searches have never been searched before. ([Google](https://twitter.com/Google/status/1493681643290300425))
6.  94.74% of keywords get 10 monthly searches or fewer. ([Ahrefs](https://ahrefs.com/blog/long-tail-keywords/))
7.  The most searched keyword in the US and globally is “YouTube,” and youtube.com gets the most traffic from Google. ([Ahrefs](https://ahrefs.com/blog/top-google-searches/))
8.  96.55% of all pages get zero search traffic from Google. ([Ahrefs](https://ahrefs.com/blog/search-traffic-study/))
9.  50-65% of all number-one spots are dominated by featured snippets. ([Authority Hacker](https://www.authorityhacker.com/million-serps-analyzed/))
10.  Reddit is the most popular domain for product review queries. ([Detailed](https://detailed.com/forum-serps/))

## Google’s Search Generative Experience (SGE) statistics

52.  AI-generated answers from SGE were available for 91% of entertainment queries but only 17% of healthcare queries. ([Statista](https://www.statista.com/statistics/1461813/google-sge-impact-by-industry/))
53.  The AI-generated answers in Google’s Search Generative Experience (SGE) do not match any links from the top 10 Google organic search results 93.8% of the time. ([Search Engine Journal](https://searchengineland.com/google-sge-links-organic-search-results-study-436275))
54.  Google displays a Search Generative element for 86.8% of all search queries. ([Authoritas](https://www.authoritas.com/blog/research-study-the-impact-of-google-sge-on-brands))  
    ![Google displays a Search Generative element for 86.8% of all search queries. ](https://ahrefs.com/blog/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/google-displays-a-search-generative-element-for-86.png)
55.  62% of generative links came from sources outside the top 10 ranking organic domains. Only 20.1% of generative URLs directly match an organic URL ranking on page one. ([Authoritas](https://www.authoritas.com/blog/research-study-the-impact-of-google-sge-on-brands))
56.  70% of SEOs said that they were worried about the impact of SGE on organic search ([Aira](https://aira.net/state-of-technical-seo/ai-machine-learning-and-seo/))
